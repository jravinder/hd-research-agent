What We've Found

Research Findings

From our AI literature agent and community research

March 2026 - Nature

AMT-130 Shows 75% Disease Slowing

First gene therapy to demonstrate meaningful disease modification in HD. 3-year data shows high-dose patients maintained more movement, thinking, and daily function. FDA demanding additional Phase III trial with sham surgery controls.

Read in Nature open_in_new
March 2026 - Labroots

Tunneling Nanotubes in HD Spread

New discovery that tunneling nanotubes play a role in how Huntington's disease spreads between cells. Could fundamentally change understanding of disease progression and open new therapeutic targets.

2026 - Novartis

Generative AI Designs 15M HD Compounds

Novartis used generative AI to computationally design 15 million potential compounds, then created predictive models for brain penetration. Narrowed to ~60 lab-tested molecules for a potent scaffold.

2026 - Unlearn

Digital Twins Could Replace Sham Surgery

Unlearn's HD Digital Twin Generator creates AI-simulated patient controls from Enroll-HD data. Could solve the FDA's demand for placebo-controlled trials without requiring sham brain surgery on healthy people.

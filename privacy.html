Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

HD Research Hub is an open-source, non-commercial research project. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every visitor. This policy explains what data we collect (very little), how we use it (only for improving the site), and your rights.

What We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (names, emails, addresses, phone numbers)
  • We do not require accounts or registration
  • We do not collect health information or medical data from visitors
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any data
  • We do not track individual visitors across sessions

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

  • Hosting analytics: Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect basic server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used solely for infrastructure monitoring and is subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Translate: If you use the translation feature, Google processes the page content to provide translation. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sources

All research data displayed on this site is sourced from publicly available databases:

  • PubMed (pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov) — Published research papers
  • ClinicalTrials.gov (clinicaltrials.gov) — Clinical trial registrations
  • HDBuzz (en.hdbuzz.net) — HD community news (via public RSS feed)
  • Open Targets (platform.opentargets.org) — Drug-target associations

We do not modify the source data. We aggregate and display it for research and educational purposes.

AI-Generated Content

Some content on this site (drug repurposing hypotheses, research scores) is generated by AI models running on our infrastructure. This content is:

  • Clearly labeled as AI-generated
  • Not a substitute for professional medical or scientific advice
  • Published for community discussion and further research
  • Generated using open-source models (Ollama/Llama) running locally — your browsing data is never sent to AI providers

Third-Party Links

This site links to external organizations (HDSA, HDBuzz, HDYO, ClinicalTrials.gov, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This site is designed for general audiences interested in HD research. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Open Source

This entire project is open source under the MIT License. You can inspect every line of code, including this privacy policy, at github.com/jravinder/hd-research-agent.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is typically nothing to delete or export. However, if you have any concerns about your privacy or data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and committed to our public GitHub repository.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue at github.com/jravinder/hd-research-agent/issues.

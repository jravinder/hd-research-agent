Purpose

HD Research Hub is an open-source, non-commercial research and educational tool. It aggregates publicly available data about Huntington's Disease research to help patients, families, researchers, and developers understand the current landscape and explore how AI might help accelerate drug discovery.

Not Medical Advice

Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendation.

  • The information presented is for research and educational purposes only
  • AI-generated hypotheses (drug repurposing candidates, research scores) are exploratory ideas, not clinical recommendations
  • Always consult qualified healthcare professionals before making any medical decisions
  • Clinical trial information is sourced from ClinicalTrials.gov and may not reflect the most current status. always verify with the trial sponsor
  • Drug repurposing candidates have NOT been clinically validated for Huntington's Disease unless explicitly noted

Data Accuracy

We make reasonable efforts to ensure accuracy by pulling from authoritative public sources (PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, HDBuzz, Open Targets). However:

  • Data is refreshed daily but may not reflect real-time changes
  • We do not independently verify every data point from source databases
  • AI-generated content may contain errors or outdated information
  • We correct errors as soon as we become aware of them

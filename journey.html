Journey

How this project came together and where it's going.

build
Milestone 1

Researched the landscape, built the platform

Scanned PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, X, Reddit, HDBuzz for everything HD-related. Found 7+ active therapies and no open-source AI research agents in the space. Built a literature agent, drug repurposing scanner (16 HD targets), trial tracker, and autoresearch loop. All running on an NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin via Ollama.

science
Milestone 2

22 papers analyzed, 5 hypotheses generated

Fed recent HD papers to Llama 3.1 8B. The LLM extracted targets and generated drug repurposing ideas. Top hypothesis: Tocilizumab for neuroinflammation (80/100). Also flagged a novel TDP-43/HD connection. Published what worked and what didn't.

rocket_launch
Milestone 3

Dashboard, chatbot, 4 autonomous agents, 46-paper corpus

Published the research dashboard with live data from 4 sources, RAG chatbot (NVIDIA NIM + Sarvam AI for Indian languages), medical guardrails, and 4 research agents (Paper Scout, Hypothesis Refiner, Social Watcher, Digest Writer). Auto-refreshes daily via GitHub Actions. 21 languages supported.
